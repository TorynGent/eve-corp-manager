EVE Corp Manager

Log in with your CEO or Director character to access live corporation data. All data is stored locally — nothing leaves your machine.

Login with EVE Online
Required ESI permissions:
  • ✓ esi-wallet.read_corporation_wallets.v1 — wallet & tax data
  • ✓ esi-corporations.read_structures.v1 — structures & fuel
  • ✓ esi-corporations.read_corporation_membership.v1 — member list
  • ✓ esi-corporations.track_members.v1 — last login tracking
  • ✓ esi-industry.read_corporation_mining.v1 — mining ledger & Metenox observers
  • ✓ esi-assets.read_corporation_assets.v1 — gas & fuel block inventory

Corp kills are fetched from zKillboard (public, no scope needed). Jita market prices are fetched anonymously from ESI.
